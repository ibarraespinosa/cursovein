metadata

A metadata [e uma planilha bem simples mas éa mais importante. Ela mostra a coluna family, vehicles, fuel, size, trips_day. A composção veicular e mostrada na coluna vehicles e representa a composição da frota brasileira.

family vehicles fuel size trips_day
PC PC_G G 1400cc 4.5
PC PC_E E 1400cc 4.5
PC PC_FG G 1400cc 4.5
PC PC_FE E 1400cc 4.5
LCV LCV_G G 3.5t 4.5
LCV LCV_E E 3.5t 4.5
LCV LCV_FG G 3.5t 4.5
LCV LCV_FE E 3.5t 4.5
LCV LCV_D D 3.5t
TRUCKS TRUCKS_SL_D D 3.8-6t
TRUCKS TRUCKS_L_D D 6-10t

TRUCKS TRUCKS_H_D D 15-40t
BUS BUS_URBAN_D D 15-18t
BUS BUS_MICRO_D D <=15t

MC MC_150_G G 150cc 4.5
MC MC_150_500_G G 150-500cc 4.5

MC MC_150_FG G 150cc 4.5
